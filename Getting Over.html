About Getting Over It

Getting Over It is a unique climbing game that tests your patience and skill. Use your hammer to climb obstacles and reach the top. The game is known for its challenging gameplay and rewarding experience.

  • Genre: Physics-based platformer
  • Developer: Bennett Foddy
  • Platform: Web browser (PC & Mobile)
  • Free to play, no download required

How to Play

  1. Use your mouse or touch to control the hammer.
  2. Climb over obstacles and try not to fall.
  3. Challenge yourself to reach the summit!
